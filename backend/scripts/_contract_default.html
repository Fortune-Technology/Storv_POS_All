3.5 Card Brand Rules & Compliance

Merchant agrees to comply at all times with the operating rules, regulations, and guidelines of all Card Brands, including without limitation Visa, Mastercard, Discover, and American Express, as amended from time to time. Merchant acknowledges that Card Brands have the right to enforce their rules directly against Merchant. Violations of Card Brand rules may result in fines, penalties, or termination of processing privileges without notice.

Merchant specifically agrees to the following:

  • Merchant shall not impose a surcharge on card transactions except where expressly permitted by applicable law AND Card Brand rules, and only with prior written approval from StoreVeu.
  • Merchant shall not set a minimum or maximum transaction amount for card payments except as permitted by Card Brand rules.
  • Merchant shall not accept card payments for goods or services not disclosed in the Merchant application, or for cash advances.
  • Merchant shall not submit a transaction for a sale that was not completed in person or authorized by the cardholder.
  • Merchant shall not split a single transaction into multiple smaller transactions to avoid authorization thresholds.
  • Merchant shall maintain PCI DSS compliance at all times and complete the required annual Self-Assessment Questionnaire.
  • Merchant shall not store full card numbers, CVV/CVC codes, or PIN data in any system or record after transaction authorization.

StoreVeu reserves the right to establish a chargeback reserve account if Merchant’s chargeback rate exceeds 1% of monthly transaction volume for two or more consecutive months. The reserve amount and terms will be communicated in writing. Excessive chargebacks may result in suspension or termination of processing services.

SECTION 4 — ADVERTISING, PLATFORM USE & BRAND RESTRICTIONS

4.1 Permitted Use of StoreVeu Name and Brand

Merchant may display “Powered by StoreVeu” or “Accepted here via StoreVeu” in their physical or digital locations. Merchant may NOT:

  • Represent themselves as a reseller, partner, agent, or affiliate of StoreVeu without a separate written reseller agreement
  • Use the StoreVeu name, logo, or brand in any advertising, social media, or marketing materials without prior written approval from StoreVeu
  • Make any claim about StoreVeu’s pricing, rates, or features that is inaccurate or misleading
  • Display StoreVeu branding in a manner that implies StoreVeu endorses or is responsible for Merchant’s products, services, or business practices

4.2 Card Brand Logo Display Requirements

Merchant must display the payment acceptance marks (Visa, Mastercard, Discover, Amex) at all points of sale where those cards are accepted, in accordance with each Card Brand’s current logo usage guidelines. Merchant must not display logos for cards they do not accept. Merchant must not display card brand logos in any manner that implies a relationship with the card brand beyond mere acceptance.

4.3 Surcharging & Dual Pricing Advertising Rules

If Merchant is enrolled in a Dual Pricing program, Merchant must:

  • Display both the cash price and the card price clearly and equally prominently at the point of sale, on receipts, and on any menu or price list
  • Ensure the card price is presented as the standard price and the cash price as a discount, not the reverse
  • Post a notice at the store entrance and at every point of sale clearly disclosing that card prices differ from cash prices
  • Never describe the card price differential as a “fee,” “surcharge,” or “processing fee” to customers in states where surcharging is prohibited (including Massachusetts)
  • Comply with all Card Brand rules for cash discount / dual pricing programs as they are updated from time to time

Merchant’s failure to comply with dual pricing advertising requirements may result in fines from Card Brands that StoreVeu is required to pass through to Merchant. StoreVeu reserves the right to immediately disable dual pricing functionality for any Merchant found to be in violation.

4.4 Platform Misuse

Merchant shall not use the StoreVeu platform for any purpose other than the lawful operation of the business described in the Merchant application. Prohibited uses include:

  • Processing transactions for goods or services not sold by Merchant
  • Processing transactions for third parties (factoring)
  • Using the platform to test or probe StoreVeu’s systems, APIs, or infrastructure
  • Attempting to access other merchants’ data through the platform
  • Using the platform to transmit malicious code, spam, or unauthorized communications
  • Circumventing or attempting to circumvent any security feature of the platform
  • Using AI-generated or automated tools to scrape, replicate, or extract data from the StoreVeu platform

Any misuse of the platform may result in immediate suspension without notice, termination of this Agreement, and StoreVeu’s pursuit of damages, including but not limited to attorney’s fees and court costs.
